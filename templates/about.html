About This Tool

Diagnosis, without the wait.

Tomato crops are vulnerable to a wide range of diseases and pests, and by the time symptoms are obvious to the eye, damage may already be spreading. Leaf & Ledger uses a transfer-learned MobileNetV2 model to recognize six common leaf conditions from a single photo, giving growers a fast first read before deciding on treatment.

How it works

A photo is resized and normalized, then passed through a MobileNetV2 network pre-trained on millions of general images and fine-tuned specifically on labeled tomato leaf photos. The model outputs a probability for each of ten possible conditions.

What it isn't

This tool offers a preliminary read, not a lab diagnosis. Confidence scores below roughly 70% or unusual lighting/backgrounds can affect accuracy. For high-stakes decisions, confirm with a local agricultural extension office.

The model

Trained on a labeled tomato leaf photo dataset, split into training and validation sets across six classes: five disease/pest categories plus healthy leaves.
